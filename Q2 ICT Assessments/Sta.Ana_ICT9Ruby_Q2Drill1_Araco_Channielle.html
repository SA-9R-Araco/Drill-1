Emergency Go Bag Essentials

Food and Water

  • Make sure that the food and water you picked are ready to eat and can withstand your hunger for 3 days
  • Do NOT use food that requres cooking (i.e. Raw poultry or meat), or food that could only last a day.

Documents

  • Important documents like your Birth certificte, License, and more should be brought with your bag.
  • Make sure they are properly protected using a ziplock or a seperate bag to prevent it from being wet.

Medkit

  • Make sure that the First aid kit contains treatments for wounds, scars, and more.
  • For example, you can put Basic first aid items like bandages, adhesive tapes, or Personal Medications that are enough for a few days.
